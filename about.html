THE MOVEMENT

Founded on the principle of creative integrity, Solace Records is more than a label. It's a refuge for the radical.

01

INDEPENDENCE

100% artist-owned content. We believe the creator should always control the vision and the legacy.

02

INNOVATION

We don't follow trends. We find the silence, the glitches, and the stories that haven't been heard yet.

03

IDENTITY

Fashion, visual art, and sound are inseparable. We build entire worlds for our artists to inhabit.

THE COLLECTIVE

Beyond the recordings, Solace exists as a global network of studios and creative labs. From our flagship headquarters in Lisbon to partner spaces in Berlin and Tokyo, we provide the infrastructure for unfiltered expression.

We are always looking for new voices that challenge the status quo. Our community thrives on collaboration, cross-genre experimentation, and the pursuit of sonic excellence.
