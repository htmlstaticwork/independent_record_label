JUNE 12, 2026 — INTERVIEW
By Alex Mercer 8 MIN READ

At Solace Records, we believe that sound is more than just frequencies—it's an immersive experience. We sit down with our artists to uncover the method behind their madness and explore the evolving landscape of modern electronic music.

"SILENCE IS THE LOUDEST INSTRUMENT."

The creative process is rarely linear. It demands dedication, an obsession with tone, and the willingness to discard everything to find that one perfect loop. In recent months, the scene has shifted dramatically toward raw, unfiltered textures that challenge conventional mixing techniques. The artists on our roster aren't just creating tracks; they are architecting audio environments.

"The studio is a sanctuary where absolute chaos is refined into pure sonic energy. You don't just listen to the final master—you feel it vibrate in the room."

— SOLACE SELECTS

Looking ahead, the commitment to high-fidelity audio remains our paramount focus. Whether it's through analog synthesis, bespoke modular rigs, or pushing digital boundaries, the goal is always to deliver an uncompromising listening experience. Stay tuned as we continue to release groundbreaking work that challenges the status quo of the global electronic scene.
